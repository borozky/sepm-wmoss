Frequently Asked Questions

Is account registration required?

No, No registration is required.

How many theaters are there in Cinema Aurora?

There are 5 Luxurious theaters available at our cinema. 2 of which are Gold class and offer variety of complementary drinks and foods.

Do you do ticket refunds??

We offer full ticket refunds to our customers, up to 1 hour before the movie start. Terms and conditions apply.

Can I buy movie tickets in advance?

Yes, You wil be able to browse and purchase tickets 1 week in advance in our 'Coming soon' page.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for the movie tickets are in AUD.
