About

Cinema Aurora Inc


Cinema Aurora is a Luxury Cinema specialising in quality mainstream and Arthouse cinema, and is located in the heart of Lygon St. We believe in the complete 'cinematic experience'- big screens, beautifully appointed seating and a warm, friendly environment. And what's more, you won't need to pay for parking.

Our graceful foyer will entice you to share a drink with a friend before viewing our selection of local and international feature films. Fancy something savory? Why not taste our delicious freshly popped popcorn included in all of our specially selected combos.

At Classic Cinema, our eclectic mix of films means every taste is catered for; from big Hollywood blockbusters to the best in foreign cinema, kids' flicks to family favorites, Classic Cinema is your perfect cinema destination.

Accessibility: Classic Cinema is fully accessible with designated seats and lift access, and there are parking spots nearby for holders of PWD parking permits.
